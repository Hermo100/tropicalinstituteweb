Who we are

TROPICAL INSTITUTE OF SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT (TISD) is Capacity Development and Consulting Firm. We offer capacity development programmes that are tailored to provide solutions that enhance the productivity, performance and profitability of our clients (Natural Resource Managers, Academia, Non-Governmental Organizations, Development Practitioners, Private Sector Actors, Civil Societies, Faith based Organizations (FBO) Cooperative Societies, and Entrepreneurs). We believe in customizing and developing specific solutions through a collaborative process designed to identify a client’s needs, requirements and objectives. We believe in a comprehensive approach–becoming a part of our client's business and having a clear understanding of their operations and challenges. We are a Solution-Driven Organization with customized program dedicated to the essence of training and development. The unique approach combined with the strong faculty of experts, competent trainers and multilingual facilitators gives our organization a competitive edge.

MISSION AND VISION

OUR MISSION is to design and deliver high impact training and development programs and a wide range of consultancy services that produce outstanding human performance in organizations

OUR VISION is To be a global leader in the provision of high quality and innovative capacity development for sustainable Development

CORE VALUES

The Institute upholds the following core values:
1.Provision of efficient and quality services
2.Integrity, Honesty, Accountability and Transparency
3.Customer responsiveness
4.Professionalism
5.Innovativeness/Creativity

GUIDING PHILOSOPHY We are committed to create a strong and continuous improvement in human capital performance with your organization. We create a smart partnership with you to fully understand your unique business strategy, goals, needs, objectives and culture. Tailor made programs and strategies are designed will to address the specific challenges facing your organization. The core aim of the capacity development is to build and sustain the skills and behaviours that are essential to your business successes.

GROWTH STRATEGY The utilization of accumulated expertise, know-how and the dynamics of its partnerships, will allow the company to positively respond to the challenges of our times and provide expert, state of the art solutions that lead straight to the point of its customer needs.

INNOVATION TISD is constantly investing to improve its state-of-the-art knowledge not only in the domains of its core expertise but also in advanced technological areas that would potentially create new business activities, enhancing its service offering.

BEST PRACTICE DISSEMINATION TISD believes that there is no substitute for learning by experience but that the sharing of experiences and lessons learned can greatly reduce risk, costs and timelines associated with sustainable development initiatives. TISD maintains a database of best practices and offers the capability of transferring best practice through training, manuals and expert coaching.

OPERATING PRINCIPLES Inclusiveness: Tropical Institute for Sustainable Development seeks to involve all bona fide stakeholders in a collective process to address the challenges of change and ensure that their views are duly considered in setting priorities for action.

Empowerment: Tropical Institute for Sustainable Development seeks to develop local capacity to the point where leadership and accountability reside with the stakeholders involved. It is the intention of TISD to make itself redundant following each engagement, through its capacity building activities.

Neutrality and transparency: Tropical Institute for Sustainable Development activities will be transparent in respect of all stakeholders.

Learning environment: Tropical Institute for Sustainable Development recognizes the challenges of sustainable development and seeks to encourage progress through learning. By promoting best practices, each new initiative can be tracked with increased confidence through shared learning.

Science-based, data-driven and beneficiary-centered processes: Tropical Institute for Sustainable Development will seek to develop consensus views based on facts/data, with ranked criteria being (i) sustainable livelihood of affected local communities and (ii) impact of activities on the environment.
